The Coaching Philosophy

Exercise Science
Meets Wholehearted Effort.

All Heart Performance isn't just a brand — it's the standard I hold myself to, and the commitment I make to every athlete I work with.

I've spent 15 years in clinical physiology and sport: understanding how the body performs under load, and how the heart adapts to it. That work runs deep enough that I help write the international exams that credential others in the field, and current enough that I draw on cutting-edge cardiovascular research.

But the science is only half of it. I've competed seriously, so I build every plan as someone who's lived the early mornings and the suffering, not just studied them. That's the idea behind the name: science you can trust, applied with heart.

What We Offer

How We Can Help

Every program begins with understanding you — your physiology, your goals, and the life you're fitting training around.

01

Personalised Programming

No templates, no guesswork. Your training plan is built from the ground up around your physiology, your schedule and the real demands of your life — so you turn up to every session knowing it's the right one.

02

Accountability & Support

A coach who notices when you're flat, celebrates when you're flying and adjusts the plan when life gets in the way — so you don't plateau, overreach, or quietly give up in week six.

03

Expert Knowledge, Individual Application

Whether it's exercise physiology, biomechanics, cardiovascular science or your own health history — deep expertise applied specifically to you, not averaged across a population. Science that means something because it's built around who you are.

04

Strategy & Decision Making

When to peak and when to train through. How to structure a year around work and family. When to push and when to recover. The big picture thinking that turns effort into progress — and keeps it sustainable long term.

05

Equipment & Gear Advice

Honest, unbiased guidance on kit, technology and training tools — cutting through the marketing noise to help you make decisions that actually serve your performance and your budget.
