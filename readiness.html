Health & Readiness

Before We Begin

A Few Questions First

This short questionnaire helps us design training that's safe and right for you. Please answer honestly — your answers are confidential, and if anything needs a closer look, we'll talk it through before you start.

If you answer Yes to any of the health-screening questions, that's not a barrier — it simply means we may ask you to check in with your doctor before we begin. When in doubt, speak with your GP.

Your details

So we know who you are and how to reach you.

Health screening

Has a doctor ever told you, or have you experienced, any of the following?

A heart condition, or that you should only do physical activity supervised by a doctor
Chest pain or discomfort during physical activity, or while at rest
Dizziness, loss of balance, or any loss of consciousness
A bone, joint or soft-tissue problem that could be made worse by activity
High blood pressure, a metabolic condition (e.g. diabetes), or medication for either
Currently pregnant, or recently given birth
Any other reason a health professional has advised you not to exercise
